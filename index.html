Cutting-Edge Technology

Progressive, Secure, High-Tech Solutions

Mobile Application

Offering the ability to book and change reservations, view trip details and receive chauffeur information.

Our mobile app is all about keeping you connected and up-to-date wherever your day takes you. When you book with LimoLink, the information you expect is available at your fingertips.

MapLink

No longer will you have to guess where your client is and when they will arrive; MapLink has you covered.

Where GPS is enabled, track the trip from your desktop, tablet, or mobile device with a real time GPS feed that shows where your chauffeur is through the duration of the trip. From the time the chauffeur arrives onsite until the passenger is dropped off at their destination, you are connected.

Real-Time Notifications

It’s fifteen minutes to pick up time and you’re wondering if your chauffeur is on site. A text from LimoLink arrives assuring you he is. Remove the doubt with our real-time notifications. Built to meet your needs, delivered the way you want them.

Client Website

Don’t have time for a phone call? Don’t worry. With LimoLink’s client website you can easily manage your reservations online, at your own leisure. Overseeing the details of your trip has never been easier. Reserve, modify and review reservations all on a secure platform that is catered to your experience.
